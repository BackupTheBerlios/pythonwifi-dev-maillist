  The best tutorials that I currently have for Python-WiFi are iwconfig.py
and iwlist.py in the Python-WiFi package.  These two scripts try to
re-implement parts of iwconfig and iwlist, but in pure Python.

  Another example appears to be lewifi at http://code.google.com/p/lewifi/

  If you get started with Python-WiFi and have questions, please don't
hesitate to ask.
