Python WiFi (PW) only provides access to the kernel management of WiFi
devices.  So, PW does not do what I guess you want.  But, you could write a
network app that used PW to handle WiFi hardware on the app host.

What I am picturing:

WNIC <--> PW <--> CoolPythonApp <--> network <--> client

PW only handles the first two items on the left.  Everything to the right
is outside the scope of PW.
